INSTRUCTION

The first game is loaded automatically, so all you have to do is click the input space and guess the correct form of the shuffled word in the grey area. If you are not sure of the answer, click the 'Random' button to get a new word, but this action can only be done once per word. If you are sure of the answer, click the 'Check' or 'Enter' button to see if your guess is correct. You have a total of 5 tries to guess the word correctly. If you want to start over, click the 'Reset' button. Good luck!
